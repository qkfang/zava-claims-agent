# Insurance Lifecycle

1. Customer gets a quote
2. Customer buys insurance
3. Policy is issued
4. Customer pays premiums
5. Customer renews, changes, or cancels policy

# Claims Lifecycle

1. Customer experiences an incident or loss
2. Customer submits a claim
3. Insurer captures claim details
4. Insurer checks policy coverage
5. Evidence and damage are assessed
6. Suppliers, repairers, or investigators may be involved
7. Settlement decision is made
8. Payment, repair, replacement, or decline is communicated
9. Claim is closed


For your demo, the story should start with:

A customer already has insurance and now needs to make a claim after something happens.

You can still show the policy as background context, because the claims agents need to check whether the customer’s policy is active and whether the loss is covered.
